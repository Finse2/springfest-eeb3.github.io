How to order

Those are the step that you should make if you want to buy foods and/or drinks from our workshop:

    1. Go to the APEEEE website between the 13th of March and Friday 22 April to order your tickets.

    2. Each ticket card costs 5 Euros and it's divided into 10 coupon worth 0.50 Euros each.

    3. After you have done all that, locate our workshop

    4. Look at the menu.

    5. Choose your food or/and drink

    6. Order

    7. Pay

    8. Grab your food and/or drinks

    9. Enjoy :)
